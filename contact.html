Out-of-Hours Enquiries

The service does not operate a formal out-of-hours service, but every attempt is made to meet urgent clinical need. For service provision outside standard hours, please arrange in advance by contacting the Clinical Scientist.

Other UK Trophoblast Centres

The UK trophoblastic disease follow-up service operates across three specialist centres:

Ninewells Hospital, Dundee

Scottish centre — HMFUS Scotland. Serves patients across Scotland.

Charing Cross Hospital, London

Major UK centre; treatment base for Scottish patients requiring chemotherapy.

Royal Hallamshire Hospital, Sheffield

Northern England centre — Sheffield Trophoblastic Disease Centre.
